## Key Financial Concepts

### Mortgages
- **Principal** - Amount borrowed
- **Interest** - Cost of borrowing
- **Amortization** - How loans are paid back over time
- **APR** - Annual Percentage Rate (includes all costs)
- **Interest Rate** - Cost of money only
- **Down Payment** - Upfront cash paid
- **PMI** - Private Mortgage Insurance (protects lender)

### Institutions & Market
- **Fannie Mae & Freddie Mac** - Secondary mortgage market
- **Lenders** - Banks, credit unions, mortgage companies
- **Servicers** - Collect payments
- **Appraisers** - Value properties
- **Realtors** - Connect buyers and sellers

### Market Terminology
- **Buyer's Market** - More homes available, buyers have leverage
- **Seller's Market** - Fewer homes available, sellers have leverage
- **Fixed-Rate Mortgage** - Rate doesn't change
- **Adjustable-Rate Mortgage (ARM)** - Rate changes periodically
- **Refinancing** - Replace existing loan with new one
